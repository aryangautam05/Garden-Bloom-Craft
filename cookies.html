Cookies Policy

Effective date: June 2024

At Garden Bloom Craft, we are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding our use of cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. This Cookies Policy provides detailed information about what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we employ, and how you can manage your preferences.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree, you may choose to decline cookies using the consent banner or adjust your browser settings accordingly.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and remember information about your visit, which can enhance your browsing experience.

Cookies perform various functions, such as remembering your preferences, enabling essential website features, and collecting analytics data. They often contain an anonymous unique identifier.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several categories of cookies on our website, described below:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the operation of our website’s core features. They enable things like page navigation, access to secure areas, and preventing security threats. Without essential cookies, the website may not function properly.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting anonymous information such as page views, time spent on pages, and navigation paths. We use this data to improve the website’s performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Functional cookies allow the website to remember choices you make, such as language preferences or login details, to personalize your experience.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies collect information about your browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements on our site and elsewhere. We currently do not use advertising cookies but will inform you if this changes.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party service providers we engage to support our website (such as analytics). These providers have their own privacy and cookie policies, and we encourage you to review those policies to understand how your data is managed.

We do not control these third-party cookies and are not responsible for their practices.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to control whether cookies are placed on your device. You can set your web browser to refuse or delete cookies. Each browser has different methods for managing cookies, which can generally be found in the “Settings” or “Preferences” menu under privacy or security.

Please note, disabling certain cookies may impact your experience and some parts of the website might not function correctly.

Additionally, you can accept or decline cookies using the consent banner located at the bottom of our website. Your choice will be stored and respected for future visits.

5. How We Use Cookies

  • To keep track of your session and authentication status while you navigate different pages.
  • To remember your preferences and personalize your experience.
  • To monitor website usage and gather analytics data to improve content and performance.
  • To maintain website security and prevent fraudulent activity.

6. Cookie Consent and Retention

Your cookie consent choice is stored securely on your device using local storage to ensure the preference is honored on future visits. If you decline cookies, non-essential cookies will not be set, but essential cookies needed for website functionality may still be used.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or adjusting your preferences through the consent banner if available.

7. Cookies on Mobile Devices

Mobile browsers generally support cookies in the same way as desktop browsers. We recommend reviewing your mobile browser settings to manage cookie usage as you would on a desktop.

8. Security and Privacy

Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information and cannot access your device’s files or other data. We employ technical measures to ensure that the data collected remains secure and used only for legitimate purposes.

Your continued use of our website after changes means you accept the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
