Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2024

Welcome to Garden Bloom Craft’s Privacy Policy page. Your privacy is critically important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and trustworthy experience while using our website and services.

This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services and personalize your experience with Garden Bloom Craft. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Identification Information: When you use our contact forms, request quotes, or get in touch, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and any other contact details you provide.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected when you visit our site, such as browser type, IP address, pages viewed, time spent, and referral sources, helping us analyze trends and improve functionality.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to store preferences, enhance site navigation, and collect analytic data. These technologies do not personally identify you but help us improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is important to us and is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries, provide quotes, and communicate about our services.
  • Process and fulfill orders or service requests.
  • Send important updates, newsletters, promotional offers, and other communications you consent to receive.
  • Monitor and analyze site usage to enhance user experience and functionality.
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws, prevent fraud, and protect our property and users.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Your Information

Garden Bloom Craft does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted partners and service providers who support our operations (e.g., web hosting, marketing, customer service). These partners are legally obligated to keep your information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the safety, rights, and property of Garden Bloom Craft, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction, with your privacy rights maintained.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Understand how you interact with our website.
  • Deliver content tailored to your interests and location.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings and opt out of tracking where applicable, but disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use encrypted connections (SSL), firewalls, secure servers, and restricted user access controls to safeguard data.

However, no internet transmission or storage method is entirely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to maintain high standards of data protection.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data, subject to applicable laws:

  • Access and Correction: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and ask us to correct any inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions in emails or contacting us directly.
  • Data Portability: Where applicable, you may request your personal data in a structured, electronic format.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services for your convenience and information. We do not control these external sites and are not responsible for their content, privacy policies, or practices.

We encourage you to review privacy policies of any third parties you engage with.

Garden Bloom Craft’s services and website are not directed at individuals under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we learn that personal data from such a child has been collected, we will take steps to delete it as soon as possible.
